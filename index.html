Lo-fi Endless Shooter

Hello.

This game was made with a lot of time, help, sanity, sleep and love ♥ from supportive friends, classmates and teachers.

I labored so that you don't have to.

This is a very simple, no-frills, endless tile game. Shoot the never-ending numbered tiles till they disappear.

Player 1: Use WASD to move around the screen but avoid hitting the tiles or your HP will drop!

Player 2 if available, hold down the left mouse button to shoot the tiles.

Turn on the music by toggling on Music ON for enhanced experience.

Or take a break and press the spacebar to pause the game.

When you're ready, click Close to be taken to the game screen. If you forget the instructions, click Help to re-read the instructions!

I hope you enjoy my simple game.
